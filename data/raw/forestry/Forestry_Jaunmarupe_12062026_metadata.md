## Description

Forest management activities (Latvian: *darbības*) planned or executed within the
Jaunmārupe project area. Sourced from the Rīgas meži forest management information
system. Each polygon represents a specific silvicultural operation applied to a
forest stand compartment in a given year.

This layer documents the management history and planned interventions relevant to
the biodiversity credit baseline and additionality assessment.

## Relevance to the Jaunmārupe project

This layer documents the silvicultural pressure on the bog woodland — particularly
clearcutting and drainage maintenance — which form part of the baseline degradation
assessment. The distribution of recent activities (2020–2026) supports the
additionality argument by showing ongoing management intensification within the
project boundary.
