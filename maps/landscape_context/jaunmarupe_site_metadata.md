## Description

Boundary of the Jaunmārupe raised bog restoration and biodiversity credit project area,
located in Mārupes novads, central Latvia. The site encompasses a degraded raised bog
(EUNIS Q54 / Natura 2000 91D0 bog woodland) with adjacent peatland habitats under active
peat extraction pressure. The project is managed by Rīgas meži (Riga Municipality Forest
Service) on behalf of Riga Municipality.

The boundary was provided by Rīgas meži and corresponds to the forest management
compartments included in the biodiversity credit application.
