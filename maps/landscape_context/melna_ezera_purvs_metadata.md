## Description

Melnā ezera purvs ("Black Lake Bog") is a Natura 2000 Special Area of Conservation
designated under the EU Habitats Directive. It is a raised bog complex located
immediately east of the Jaunmārupe project boundary, forming part of the same
continuous peatland landscape. Together with Cenas Tīrelis to the west, it
provides the ecological connectivity context for the Jaunmārupe biodiversity
credit application.

## Relevant habitat types (Annex I)

- **7110** — Active raised bogs (priority habitat)
- **7120** — Degraded raised bogs still capable of natural regeneration
- **91D0** — Bog woodland (priority habitat)
