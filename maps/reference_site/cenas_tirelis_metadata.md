## Description

Cenas Tīrelis is a Natura 2000 Special Area of Conservation (SAC) designated under
the EU Habitats Directive. It is a large raised bog complex directly adjacent to and
partially overlapping the Jaunmārupe project area, forming a continuous peatland
landscape in central Latvia. The site is one of the best-preserved raised bogs in
the Riga region and provides the ecological context for restoration connectivity
arguments in the Jaunmārupe biodiversity credit application.
